FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this calculator show?+
It shows your real out-of-pocket cost after subtracting all scholarships, grants, and other financial aid from your total college cost.
What counts as financial aid?+
Anything that reduces your bill: merit scholarships, need-based grants, federal grants like Pell, university aid, departmental awards, and external scholarships.
Should I include loans?+
No. Loans must be repaid, so they should not be counted as aid. This calculator focuses on free money like scholarships and grants.
Is my data saved?+
No. All calculations happen in your browser. Nothing is stored or shared.
