FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is CGPA?+
CGPA stands for Cumulative Grade Point Average. It represents the weighted average of GPAs from all semesters you have completed, weighted by the credit hours of each semester.
How is CGPA different from GPA?+
GPA is the average for a single semester. CGPA is the cumulative average across all semesters you have completed so far.
How is CGPA calculated?+
Multiply each semester GPA by its total credit hours, sum those values, and divide by the total credit hours from all semesters.
Is my data saved?+
No. Calculations happen in your browser. Nothing is stored or shared.
