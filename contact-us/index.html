FAQ

Common Questions

Before reaching out, check if your question is already answered below.

How quickly do you respond to messages?+
We typically respond within 24 to 48 hours on business days. We read every message and do our best to get back to you as quickly as possible.
Can I suggest a new calculator or tool?+
Absolutely! We love hearing from students about what tools would help them most. Use the contact form above, select "Tool Suggestion" as the subject, and describe what you need. We consider every suggestion seriously.
Do you offer technical support for the calculators?+
Yes. If a calculator is not working correctly or you have found a bug, please describe the issue in the contact form — including which calculator, what you were doing, and your device or browser — and we will look into it promptly.
