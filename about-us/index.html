Our Story

Why We Built StudentCalcTools

StudentCalcTools started in 2024 out of a simple frustration: why were the most useful academic tools either locked behind paywalls, cluttered with ads, or difficult to understand? Students were spending more time figuring out how to use a GPA calculator than actually calculating their GPA.

We set out to fix that. Starting with a clean, fast GPA calculator, we gradually expanded into a full suite covering grades, attendance, final grades, CGPA, student loans, and more. Every tool was designed mobile-first because most students reach for their phone when they need a quick answer between classes.

Today, StudentCalcTools serves students across the globe — from high schoolers checking their GPA for college applications to university students managing complex credit-hour calculations. We're a small, dedicated team that puts students first, and we're just getting started.
