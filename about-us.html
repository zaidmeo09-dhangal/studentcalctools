Our Mission

StudentCalcTools was created to give every student access to free, accurate, and easy to use academic calculators. Whether you need to calculate your GPA, track your attendance, plan your study time, or figure out your final grade — we have a tool for that.

We believe that useful academic tools should be free and accessible to every student in the world, not locked behind paywalls or subscriptions.
