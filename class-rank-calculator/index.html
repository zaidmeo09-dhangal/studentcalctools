How Percentile Is Calculated

Percentile shows what percent of students rank below you. Formula: ((Total − Rank + 1) ÷ Total) × 100. Higher percentile means you rank above more peers.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is class rank percentile calculated?+
Percentile = ((Total Students − Your Rank + 1) ÷ Total Students) × 100. A higher percentile means you rank above more classmates.
What is a good class rank percentile?+
Top 10 percent (90th percentile and above) is excellent. Top 25 percent is very competitive for college admissions.
How does class rank affect college admissions?+
Many colleges consider class rank along with GPA and test scores. Top 10 percent is often a key threshold for selective universities.
Is my data saved?+
No. All calculations happen in your browser. Nothing is stored or shared.
