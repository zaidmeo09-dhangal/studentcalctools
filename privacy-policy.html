Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Welcome to StudentCalcTools. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Usage data such as pages visited, time spent, and browser type
  • Device information including IP address and operating system
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information. All our tools work entirely in your browser and no calculation data is stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Improve our website and tools
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Monitor website performance and fix issues

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track website traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics collects anonymous data about how visitors use our site. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

4. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can instruct your browser to refuse cookies, but some parts of our site may not function properly as a result.

6. Third Party Links

Our website may contain links to third party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to read their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is designed for students of all ages but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@studentcalctools.com
