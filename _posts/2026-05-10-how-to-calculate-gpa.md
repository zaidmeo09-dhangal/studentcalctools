GPA calculation is easier when you understand three things: credit hours, grade points, and the GPA formula.

## What Is GPA?

GPA stands for Grade Point Average. It shows your academic performance across your courses.

## GPA Formula

```text
GPA = Total Grade Points / Total Credit Hours
```

## Simple Example

If a student earns 12 grade points across 4 credit hours, the GPA is 3.0.
