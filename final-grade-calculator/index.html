FORMULA

How This Final Grade Calculator Works

This final grade calculator works backward from your target grade. It uses your current grade, your target grade, and the final exam weight to calculate the score you need on the final.

Formula Required Final Exam Score = (Target Grade - Current Grade × (1 - Final Exam Weight)) ÷ Final Exam Weight

Enter the final exam weight as a percentage in the calculator. In the formula, the weight is treated as a decimal.

Example

Current grade: 82%

Target grade: 90%

Final exam weight: 25%

Required score = (90 - 82 × 0.75) ÷ 0.25

Required score = 114%

That means a 90% overall grade may not be possible unless your class allows extra credit or your teacher uses a different grading rule.

RESULTS

What If Your Required Score Is Too High or Too Low?

What if you need more than 100%?

If the calculator shows a required score above 100%, your target grade may not be mathematically possible with regular grading. This can happen when your current grade is much lower than your target grade, the final exam weight is too small, or your target grade is very high.

What if you need less than 0%?

If the calculator shows a required score below 0%, you have already reached your target grade mathematically. You should still complete your final exam unless your teacher, school, or syllabus says otherwise.

Can I use this as a final exam grade calculator?+
