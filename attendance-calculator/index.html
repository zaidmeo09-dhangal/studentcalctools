FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is attendance percentage calculated?+
Attendance percentage is calculated by dividing the number of classes attended by the total number of classes held, then multiplying by 100.
What is a safe attendance percentage?+
Most colleges and schools require a minimum of 75 percent attendance. Maintaining 80 percent or higher is considered safe.
How many classes can I miss?+
This calculator tells you exactly how many more classes you can miss while staying above your target attendance percentage.
Is my data saved?+
No. All calculations happen in your browser. Nothing is stored or shared.
